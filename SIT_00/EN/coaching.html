SERVICES : COACHING

Coaching mainly focuses on motivating you towards a positive transition. It focuses on the ‘here and now’ and look ahead rather than on the past. Fundamentally, the coach is helping individuals to solve their own problems and improve their own performance. Unlike Psychotherapy, the coachee should always have a specific personal or professional goal with clear timeline.
Some examples of who can benefit from Coaching are:
 Wants to acquire new skills or qualification
 Wants to change one’s job
 Wants to overcome personal obstacles
 Wants to reflect on one’s values to find a job / life happiness

If you’re feeling stuck in life or work and are ready to change without having mental health condition, past trauma, or unprocessed emotions, coaching maybe more suitable. The coach would help you manage existing stress and move forward in life.
