MODE OF THERAPY

 In-Person

This is still the most traditional and effective mode of therapy. In-person therapy, you meet with a therapist / psychologist at a specified time in private and confidential settings – private office and speak face to face. The benefit of this mode is that you and the therapist can have a real-time conversation, where they can pick up on your body language and tone of voice, which can give them insights on what you're feeling.

 Video Call

Web-based online therapy is ideal if you want an experience that is comparable to seeing a therapist in person. You will be able to see each other’s body language and hear each other’s tone of voice. It is most suitable for clients who need help urgently, has time constraint or geographically distant from the office. We accommodate the platform of your preference such as Microsoft Teams, Zoom, Google Meet, or FaceTime.

 Voice Call

Using your mobile phone app such as WhatsApp Telegram, you can speak our therapist through voice calls, similar to talking on a phone. This allows for tone and emotions to come through to your therapist, although you will not see each other’s faces.
